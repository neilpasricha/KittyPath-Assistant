Getting Started

In order to use this extentsion, you must logon to Lionpath and navigate to the degree
audit. Once you have arrived at your degree audit, open the extension, and click the
Degree Audit Button. If you are having trouble accessing your degree audit, follow
this Link for instructions.

Clicking on the Degree Audit Button in KittyPath Assistant will allow you to view your
GPA, unfufilled requirements, your current classes, and classes not being used to fufill
your degree.

Search

Searching for classes is easy in KittyPath. You can search for classes based on the
requirement it satisfies or by the course ID. Simply, click the search tab and select how
you would like to search. Once you have found the course, you can save it to your
Course selction by pressing save.

Plan

To view an academic plan, choose your current campus and then select if you are a
two plus two student.
